# Itzhak Bentov — Stalking the Wild Pendulum: YouTube Documentary

## Overview

An independent YouTube documentary presenting Bentov's core ideas from _Stalking the Wild Pendulum_ with commentary, diagrams, and personal narrative from the presenter. It covers the same intellectual territory as the _New Heaven New Earth_ programme but from a different angle — more irreverent in tone, more explicit about Bentov's CIA connections, and interwoven with the presenter's own meditative experiences. Original music (apparently written and performed by the presenter) ends the documentary.

## Background — Bentov and the CIA

The documentary opens with biographical context most sources omit:

Bentov survived **Nazi concentration camps** before emigrating and working as a mechanical engineer and biomedical inventor. His technical work on instruments to study physiology in altered states of consciousness caught the attention of the **CIA**, where he became a significant contributor to what are now partially **declassified documents** — associated with programmes like **Project Stargate** and the **Gateway Process** (the Monroe Institute protocols used by the CIA to train remote viewers).

His specialty: _helping people transcend the illusion of physical flesh and bear witness to the infinite nature of the universe for themselves._

His book _Stalking the Wild Pendulum_ was published while the CIA documents were still classified. After publication, he shared the underlying ideas openly — never disclosing CIA work specifically, but not hiding what he knew.

He died **25 May 1979** aboard American Airlines Flight 191, which crashed shortly after takeoff from Chicago. The presenter notes — without asserting foul play — that a striking number of people involved in these now-declassified programmes died in similar accidents. "When they are done with you, they are done with you." He notes the death was "as public as it gets."

_"If you want zero literature, read the words of men who died early."_

## Cymatics — Sound as the Foundation of Reality

The documentary introduces **cymatics** (the study of sound-frequency patterns) as the key to understanding Bentov's model:

Reality is founded in **sound**. Sound is an oscillation — movement that comes to rest, then swings back, like a pendulum. These oscillations cause **vibratory patterns** that we call physical reality.

**The cymatics experiment**: Sprinkle sand on a metal plate. Vibrate the plate at a particular frequency. The sand avoids the vibrating areas (nodes) and clumps in the areas of least motion (antinodes), forming **precise geometric patterns**. The faster the frequency, the more complex the pattern. As frequency increases: simple lines → more complex grids → increasingly intricate mandalic symmetries.

This is **chaos into order** — static, chaotic sand interacting with sound frequency to organise into geometric structure.

**The holographic element of cymatics**: Only a small portion of the pattern is necessary to understand the whole. You can predict what an entire snowflake looks like from a tiny fragment. Similarly, you can understand the entire universe by looking within — "all the world is in a grain of sand" (Buddhist teaching, Blake's poem).

The **friction** between the two states (rest and motion) is what gives birth to physical reality. The suffering stemming from that friction is what causes **evolution and growth**.

## The Three-Pebbles Hologram Experiment

Bentov's famous demonstration:

Three pebbles dropped simultaneously into a bowl of water. Each creates ripples. The ripples from all three interfere with each other, creating a complex **interference pattern** on the surface. This pattern contains accurate information about the **position and motion** of the pebbles — even though the pebbles themselves are now invisible beneath the surface.

Analogously: the interference pattern of reality contains raw information about everything Unseen. We can extrapolate the entire hidden world from the surface pattern.

A Zen master asked modern science: _"Why do you spend all this time and money on rockets and microscopes when you can understand the universe by sitting and watching a creek flow around some stones?"_

Stresses cause irregularities in this harmony. As soon as the source of disturbance is removed, orderly rhythm returns. The presenter: _"Add joke about governments here."_

Analogy: take a completely still, undisturbed body of water — it is transparent, as if nothing is there. Disturb the surface and you can suddenly see the water. The void gives way to visible reality through motion. The depths remain still.

Bentov stated: when we magnify physical reality to examine what it is made of, we find a **rapidly pulsating matrix of fields of energy** and interference patterns of waves filling a vacuum — outward forever.

## The 7 Hz Oscillation — Matter Blinking

The so-called particles composing our bodies **blink in and out of existence** — oscillating 14 times per second (7 up, 7 down). This is why the electromagnetic field of the human body vibrates at **7 Hz**.

At the "precipice and valley" of this oscillation — the points of rest at either extreme — there is a **tiny moment where reality blinks out**. This happens so fast we are unaware of it in normal waking consciousness.

Through meditative practice, one can **extend the subjective time of those blinks** — spending more conscious time in the out-interval, exploring higher realities, then returning.

7 Hz is also a **harmonic of the Earth's Schumann resonance** (~7.83 Hz). This ability to tune into the Earth's frequency is not unique to humans:

- Birds use it to navigate thousands of miles without GPS
- Whales use it to locate each other across ocean distances

When the Earth's field is disturbed — solar flares, tectonic events — whales beach themselves and birds fly into the ground. The same stress buildup in the nervous system happens to humans. Yoga and meditation alleviate this buildup.

## Reality as Motion — The Tibetan Teaching

From _Secret Teachings of Tibetan Buddhist Sects_ (Alexandra David-Neel):

> _"The tangible world is movement, not a collection of moving objects, but movement itself. There are no objects in movement; it is the movement which constitutes the objects which appear to us. The movement is a continued and infinitely rapid succession of flashes of energy. Objects perceptible to our senses are constituted by a rapid succession of instantaneous events."_

Two theories of movement: (1) continuous, like a river; (2) intermittent — advancing by separate flashes of energy at intervals so small as to be nearly non-existent. The second aligns with Bentov.

This raises the question: **where does matter go when it periodically disappears?**

## Time Dilation — Bentov's Technique

Time is subjective — Einstein: _"When you spend two hours with a pretty girl you think it's only a minute. When you sit on a hot stove for a minute you think it's two hours."_

Bentov's written technique for experiencing subjective time dilation:

1. Relax. Place a watch in front of you.
2. Follow the second hand in a relaxed way. Absorb and remember its rhythm.
3. Close your eyes. Visualise yourself engaged in your most favourite, relaxing activity — **be there**, not just think about it. Feel the warmth of the sun, the texture of the sand. Perfect vivid embodied visualisation.
4. When the visualisation has stabilised, slowly open your eyes just a bit. Don't focus on the watch — let your gaze fall on the dial as a disinterested observer.
5. If done correctly: the second hand will slow, stick in places, hover. Very successful practitioners can **stop the second hand** for an extended period. The moment of shock at seeing this causes the second hand to resume normal speed.

The presenter admits this technique does not work for him ("you put a lump of coal in my butt, you'll have a diamond in two weeks"). His own method: the **Wim Hof breathing technique**, specifically the breath-hold phase, which creates a state of non-thought in which he can perceive self and consciousness directly. He has experienced 1–2 minutes feeling like 15–20 minutes. He describes it as "a sort of superpower and mental clarity and an immunity to pain."

## Consciousness Defined Quantitatively

Bentov's scientific definition:

_"Consciousness is the capacity of a system to respond to stimuli."_

Spectrum of response:

- Atom: stimulate with ultraviolet light → electrons jump to higher orbits (a few possible responses)
- Bacterium: tickle it → larger number of responses
- Complex organism: even more varied, more numerous responses

The **quantity of consciousness** = the number of possible response types a system can produce.

This removes the arbitrary line between "living" and "non-living." Minerals have difficulty understanding human consciousness just as we have difficulty understanding theirs. The human habit of defining life as "things that can reproduce" is projection — we recognise our own behaviour in other systems and call it life; we don't recognise it in minerals and call them dead.

**Humans, when properly trained, can interact with anything that has consciousness at whatever level** — because the bandwidth of the human nervous system (expressed as bands F5–F6 in Bentov's diagram) can be extended.

The bands above human (F7, F8) represent realities for populations occupying those levels. All matter in the universe, from atoms upward, is evolving under the force of evolution toward the Absolute — graduating step by step through each band.

## Thought, Intuition, and the Bicycle Parable

Thought is a **coarse and jagged thing**. It is a function of the brain, not necessarily of the mind. The brain amplifies the signal of pure Consciousness (the Absolute) into the form of thought — useful for survival, but limited.

Beyond thought: **intuition**. In higher, uncharted areas of mind, intuition is more reliable than cold logic.

Bentov illustrates with the **bicycle parable**: if you show someone who has never encountered a bicycle a diagram of how it works, they may laugh — a thin seat on two unstable wheels, easily toppled. What they are missing is the **invisible principle of inertia** that keeps the bicycle upright when in motion. This cannot be explained; it can only be _experienced_. This is **gnosis** — knowledge through direct experience rather than intellectual explanation.

## The Soul vs. Spirit

The **soul** (parallel to the psychological term _psyche_) is independent of the body. It uses the body as a "garage" — a place of learning. Soul ≠ spirit; they are distinct.

The universe wants to make itself known to those who can comprehend its language. This emanation of the universe toward the comprehensible is described as **love**.

Bentov posits: **love and gravitation may be the same phenomenon**. The presenter references a passage in _Zosimos_ (an alchemical/Gnostic text) in which the master "had mastered all the great mysteries before him except for gravitation — as he had explained that it pervades and pierces all things in the universe, whether physical or not." Love similarly has a gravitational effect — it pulls toward itself across all levels.

_"The whole universe is love and is an information-gathering system."_

## Reincarnation as Oscillation

Applying the law of oscillation to the gradient of consciousness: there is a **spiralling pendulum of motion** that moves up and forward with each cycle of death and rebirth. If everything in nature follows the oscillation of cycles, the concept of death and rebirth follows suit. It would be stranger if it did not — _"a rare case where Occam's Razor seems to be working in the favour of life after death and rebirth."_

These levels of consciousness overlap and differ depending on the sensitivity of each individual's nervous system. This is why some people can communicate with animals (some better than others), some with plants, and shamans can communicate with consciousness levels in the causal realms above.
