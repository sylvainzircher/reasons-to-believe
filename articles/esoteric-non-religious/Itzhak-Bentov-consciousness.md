# Itzhak Bentov — Consciousness, Evolution, and the Mechanics of Reality

## Overview

Two-segment programme: the first is a live interview with **Itzhak Bentov** ("Ben") on the television show _New Heaven New Earth_, discussing the evolution of consciousness and miracles reinterpreted as neurological events. The second is a posthumous presentation of Bentov's complete model — drawn from his two books _Stalking the Wild Pendulum: On the Mechanics of Consciousness_ and _A Cosmic Book: On the Mechanics of Creation_ — narrated by a presenter who knew him. Together they constitute a comprehensive introduction to Bentov's worldview: a synthesis of biomedical engineering, quantum physics, Eastern mysticism, and direct mystical experience.

**Itzhak Bentov** (full pronunciation: ee-TZAHK ben-TOV) was born in Czechoslovakia, trained as a mechanical engineer, later became a biomedical inventor and researcher. He began meditating and his consciousness underwent expansion that he interpreted structurally, as an engineer. He described himself as "a nuts and bolts man — a plumber." He illustrated both his books with his own cartoon-like drawings. He died in the American Airlines Chicago crash of 1979.

## Part 1: Live Interview — The Evolution of Consciousness

### Where Are We in Evolution?

Bentov's starting framework is Darwinian but he extends it: accepting that we have evolved from ape-like ancestors to the current bipedal, moderately intelligent human, he asks where this trajectory leads. He uses the **bell curve** (normal distribution) as his explanatory tool.

The bell curve of any population plots the frequency of traits. For human consciousness today:

- The far left tail: "gorilla-type" people — reactive, chest-beating, dominating
- The bulk of the curve: the average modern human — drives cars, pushes buttons, reasonably functional
- The far right tail: very highly evolved people — a tiny minority

Evolution, Bentov argues, pushes the entire curve to the right over time. In half a million to a million years, the current right-tail (rare geniuses, mystics) will be the new average. The new cutting edge will be entities we cannot currently imagine — possibly without physical bodies.

**Where do you find today's right-tail people?** The interviewer suggests universities and leading professions. Bentov corrects: _"Mental hospitals and nap houses."_ The reason: people at the cutting edge of evolution live in a _different reality_ — one that most people cannot perceive. Unable to integrate this expanded perception into daily functioning, they appear disturbed. Those who _can_ integrate it become what we call geniuses: Newton, Darwin, Leonardo da Vinci.

### What Is Evolving — The Nervous System

The mechanism of evolution is the **nervous system**, supported by the skeleton. Our senses — eyes, ears, nose, skin — are extensions of the nervous system, extensions of the brain. And all senses are radically limited:

- Vision: only the narrow visible spectrum (no UV, no infrared)
- Hearing: 20 Hz to 20,000 Hz maximum
- Every other sense: similarly band-limited

We therefore see reality through "a very narrow slit in the total reality." As the nervous system evolves, this slit widens. We see more of the one large reality. Evidence: the Bible describes only 4–6 colours (the perceptual range of 4,000 years ago); modern humans see hundreds of thousands of colours. Our fellow mammals (cows, horses) see in black and white. This is the evolution of the nervous system in action.

### Miracles Reinterpreted — The Spontaneous Opening

Bentov introduces the concept of **spontaneous opening** — a sudden, involuntary expansion of sensory range to previously imperceptible reality. In earlier times this was called a miracle, a vision, a revelation.

**Example 1 — The teenager at dinner**: A 15-year-old looks up and sees his dead grandmother standing in the corner. His mother cannot see her. He is taken to a psychiatrist. The psychiatrist cannot see her either. He is prescribed Thorazine (or electroshock). In two weeks he no longer sees anything. The process has been reversed. This is labelled a "psychotic episode" or "schizophrenic break."

Bentov's interpretation: there is a _good chance_ that the teenager had a spontaneous opening of his senses — his evolutionary development was temporarily accelerated. Rather than recognising and nurturing it, modern psychiatry suppresses it.

**Example 2 — St Paul on Route 128**: St Paul, riding to Damascus, is blinded by a ball of white light containing Jesus, who rebukes him. He falls, makes it to Damascus, builds the church, and the Judeo-Christian tradition results.

Now suppose the same event happened to a modern person driving on Route 128 (Boston's highway). The ball of light descends in front of his windshield. His driving becomes "wobbly." A police officer pulls him over. He keeps babbling about Jesus. He is arrested for driving under the influence, transferred to hospital, and logged as arriving with "acute psychotic episode — religious overtones."

**Conclusion**: _"This is what we did to the miracle. We took the miracle and we ground it to grey powder."_ Everyone complains there are no more miracles. There are — we just institutionalise them.

### What Is the Soul

The interview turns to what else, beyond the nervous system, is evolving. Bentov challenges the common understanding of the soul:

_"Most people go to church, take the soul out of the closet, polish it up a little bit, then come home and put it back until next week."_

His correction: we don't _have_ souls. **A soul has us.** The soul is the permanent, eternal entity. The body is disposable — like a car driven for 80,000–100,000 miles, then scrapped. The soul (the driver) gets another one. It is the soul that is experiencing evolution across multiple lifetimes. The body and personality are temporary. The soul is the repository of all information gathered across lifetimes.

### The Levels Model (First Version)

Bentov sketches a multi-level model of being:

**Physical level**: Individuals are completely separate. You are there, I am here.

**Soul level**: Each person's soul extends outward, nearly to infinity. At this level the souls of two people _overlap_ — they touch and mingle. Separation is diminished.

**Higher Self level** (the "boss of the soul"): Extension is even greater; overlap between individuals is much larger.

**Highest spiritual level**: Complete overlap. Everyone is everywhere simultaneously. This is **omnipresence** — the state of perfected beings, of what various traditions call Gods.

All these levels exist simultaneously. We exist on all of them simultaneously — we are just not aware of it. The perception of separate entities is only the view from the physical level. Whether we like it or not, all of us are evolving toward this state of omnipresence, of godhood. _"It takes eons — don't hold your breath."_

### Purpose of Evolution

The system (reality, consciousness, the cosmos) is an **intelligence- and information-gathering system** that also freely distributes information. The end state toward which evolution drives is a state of omniscience and omnipresence — knowing the system from within, as the system. This is the purpose.

One can accelerate this by meditation (which pushes the nervous system faster than the normal evolutionary rate) — but it will happen anyway. One does not need to do anything.

### The Physiology of Expanded Consciousness — Kundalini Model

Bentov, as a biomedical engineer, designed instruments to study physiological changes when consciousness expands. His key discovery: the **ballistograph** experiment.

A subject sits on a chair equipped with a ballistograph (a device measuring microvibrations). At rest:

- Large curve = breathing motion (body moving up and down with breath)
- Small wiggly lines superimposed = heartbeat (irregular)

In deep meditation:

- Breathing practically disappears — near straight line
- The heartbeat transforms into a **regular sine wave**

An engineer's interpretation: the body has entered a state of **harmonisation of all frequencies** — it is resonating like a tuned instrument.

The sine wave frequency is **7 Hz** (cycles per second) — also called 7 Hertz.

**3. Third ventricle of the brain**: A fluid-filled cavity in the brain. The regular 7 Hz vibration creates a **standing wave** in the ventricle's fluid. On top of the third ventricle is a drum-like surface with a **bundle of nerve fibres crossing it** — the corpus callosum (connecting the right and left hemispheres). **5. Electromagnetic field of the head**: The cumulative result of all four oscillators is an **electromagnetic field around the head** — the head becomes an antenna, capable of sending and receiving information it could not otherwise access. In this state, higher perceptions become possible.

### The Nature of Physical Reality — The Pendulum Analogy

Bentov's core physical model: **reality is nothing but oscillating fields in the void**.

**The atom examined**: If you magnify the atom and zero in on the electron, all you find is an oscillating electromagnetic field. No solid matter. The nucleus is the same. Between the nucleus and the electron there is only void — the same void as interstellar space. The ratio of distance would be analogous to a pinhead in the middle of a room with something rotating 30 feet away — and the rest is void.

**The pendulum**: Bentov draws an analogy between the electron (seen alternately on each side of the nucleus) and a pendulum. A pendulum: action → rest → action → rest. If you attach a pen to a swinging pendulum and draw paper beneath it, you get a **sine wave**. Reality IS the spaces between the points of rest (the motion). At the points of rest (where the pendulum stops before reversing), we perceive reality. Where there is no change — total rest — reality disappears because we cannot perceive it.

Physical matter is the **interaction between oscillating fields** — the reason you cannot walk through a floor is not because the floor is solid, but because the electrons in your body's atoms **repel** the electrons in the floor's atoms. If you could speed up your vibration sufficiently, you could pass through the floor. Matter is an **illusion of solidity** produced by electromagnetic repulsion.

### The Neuron — How Subjective Reality Is Constructed

The nervous system perceives the oscillating physical world through its own oscillating mechanism. A neuron at rest sits at baseline. When stimulated — by anything, a loud noise, a bright light — it fires: a spike on the baseline. More spikes = more intense stimulus. The brain then _constructs_ reality from these spikes (which merely say "I am being disturbed"). From this binary signal, we construct flowers, animals, music, sky.

Our senses are therefore radically incomplete: dogs hear ultrasonic; bees see UV. We perceive through a very narrow slot. **One oscillating system (our nervous system) is looking at another oscillating system (physical reality) — the picture is very blurred indeed.**

We extend our reality with instruments (telescopes, microscopes) but ultimately it is still our senses that give us the knowledge.

### The Heisenberg Uncertainty Connection

Heisenberg's uncertainty principle: when the momentum (speed) of a particle is precisely known, its position becomes unknowable — and vice versa. Applied to the pendulum: at the points of rest, the pendulum's speed is known (zero), therefore its _position becomes unknowable_. Bentov: at those points of rest, **the pendulum can be found anywhere in the universe** — it becomes the universe, it fills all creation.

Applied to consciousness: we too oscillate like a pendulum. At the points of rest in our oscillation we **touch the Absolute state** — briefly, so briefly we are not aware of it. But if we could expand that moment of rest and inhabit it consciously, we would expand into the Absolute state — experiencing it while remaining simultaneously aware of our normal reality. This is what meditation achieves progressively.

### Ben's Model of the Universe — The Torus

Bentov modified the standard Big Bang model based on observations of **quasars** (discovered in the 1960s — radio galaxies of enormous size, the oldest observed objects, many showing a jet emerging from the center). Quasars are the most distant, fastest-moving objects ever observed.

Bentov noticed that quasars are **not evenly distributed** in the observable universe — they are more concentrated toward the North and South Galactic Poles, and slightly offset (about 30°) from the true pole. This **anisotropy** (uneven distribution) contradicts a simple isotropic Big Bang.

His model: instead of an isotropic explosion in all directions, the original Cosmic Egg **exploded in one direction** — like a balloon pricked with a needle — producing a **jet**. With nothing around it, the jet's own gravity eventually pulls it back, creating a current. The structure produced is a **torus** (a doughnut or toroidal shape).

**The Torus model**:

- Matter emerges from the **white hole** (the center) in the same sequence as the Big Bang: light radiation → photons → stable particles → gases (helium, hydrogen) → stars → galaxies
- Matter expands outward and around the torus
- It condenses into galaxies which are eventually pulled by gravity back into the **black hole**
- In the singularity between white hole and black hole, matter undergoes transmutation — gravitational collapse to extreme density — and re-emerges as energy, beginning another circuit
- The structure is **continuous** — a "modest but continuous bang" — not a one-time explosion
- Each time matter completes a circuit, its Consciousness is **refined** — it emerges on the next round of the spiral at a higher level of consciousness

The Milky Way's position: quasars being found off the true galactic pole in the South suggests we may be looking back toward the jet — we are at a position where matter is just entering the expansion phase. This expansion of matter _is_ an expansion of consciousness — which may explain the accelerating changes in human consciousness currently being experienced.

The torus is "a distilling column of Consciousness" — refining consciousness as matter is recirculated.

**Nature repeats patterns**: Bentov attached electrodes to a chicken egg (similar in shape to the torus) and measured a potential of 2.4 millivolts. Every seed, every egg in nature has a field around it. The same design appearing at different scales.

**Information**: Every particle of matter on the torus surface sends out information about itself. All the information from all matter everywhere gets interwoven into one web — the **universal mind hologram**. In higher states of consciousness one can tap this hologram and access information about the entire structure — because we are part of it (a little sliver of the hologram that still retains all the information).

**Time**: Linear time is born as matter issues from the white hole and begins its circuit. It ends as matter returns to the black hole. Viewed from outside (from another dimension, from the eternal), this linear time is a very limited phenomenon — a continuum, not a beginning and end.
