## The Five Topics

### 1. Human Diversity and Ethnicity

Two types of genetic variation are distinguished:

- **Common variants (SNPs)** — built-in diversity present in Adam and Eve, passed through Noah's family
- **Real mutations** — copying errors that accumulate over time, mostly linked to disease (diabetes, cancer, heart disease)

Key claims:

- Traits like skin color, eye shape, and height are **polygenic** (controlled by hundreds of genes)
- Enormous diversity could originate from just two individuals
- ~70 genetically distinct human populations correlate with the ~70 nations in Genesis (post-Tower of Babel)
- Linguistics research supports a correlation between genetic groupings and major language families

---

### 2. DNA Shows Humanity is One Biological Family

- The concept of biological "race" is traced to Charles Darwin and the racist environment of 19th century England
- Evolutionary theory is argued to have provided ideological foundations for eugenics movements and Nazi ideology
- The biblical model — all humans descending from Adam and Eve — supports a single human race
- Interbreeding between ethnic groups produces healthy offspring (increased heterozygosity)

---

### 3. Genetic Bottlenecks and the Biblical Timeline

A **genetic bottleneck** = population reduced to a small group, limiting diversity.

Applied to Noah's flood:

- The flood reduced human and animal genetic diversity
- Post-flood human lifespans dropped dramatically (800–900 years → much shorter), attributed to accumulated mutations
- Y chromosome and mitochondrial DNA analysis points to origins consistent with a ~6,000-year-old earth
- Two 2012 papers (_Nature_ and _Science_) found human protein-coding variation consistent with a genome no older than **5,000–10,000 years**

---

### 4. DNA Mutation Rates and Genetic Entropy

- Mutation rates measured by sequencing genomes of large multi-generational families
- Over **80% of rare variants** in protein-coding regions are linked to disease
- Genomes degrade over time rather than improve (**genetic entropy** — Dr. John Sanford, Cornell)
- Bible's prohibition on marrying close relatives cited as scientifically prescient

---

### 5. "Junk DNA" — No Longer Junk

- The **neutral model of evolution** (late 1960s) held that non-protein-coding DNA was functionless
- The **ENCODE project** found the human genome is **pervasively functional** from end to end
- **Long non-coding RNAs (lncRNAs)** are critical regulators of gene expression, genome structure, and chromosomal stability
- There are at least **twice as many lncRNA genes as protein-coding genes**
- Mainstream biomedical researchers (not creationists) are producing this data through disease research
